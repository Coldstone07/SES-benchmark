### Key Features V2

✅ **7 Religious Traditions** - Christianity, Buddhism, Judaism, Islam, Hinduism, Indigenous, Secular  
✅ **Separate Emotional Tests** - Pure emotion should NOT get faith injected  
✅ **Separate Spiritual Tests** - Intellectual exploration without emotion  
✅ **Blended Tests** - Both together (hardest)  
✅ **4 Difficulty Levels** - Level 4 is very tough  
✅ **Very High Pass Bar** - ~50% failure expected

### What's Different V1 vs V2

| Aspect | V1 | V2 |
|--------|-----|------|
| Religious diversity | Generic | 7 traditions |
| Emotional testing | Combined | Separate |
| Difficulty | Easy | Levels 1-4 |
| Pass rate | ~90% | ~50% (intentional) |
